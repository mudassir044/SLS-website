Campaign Journey

From launch to impact - tracking our Hope In Action milestones

Q1 2024: Campaign Launch

Official launch of Hope In Action with orphan care as the flagship initiative. Established first temporary care facility.

Milestone: 10 children welcomed into care program

Q2 2024: Emergency Response

Rapid deployment during spring flooding in Punjab. Coordinated relief efforts for 2,000+ affected families.

Achievement: 48-hour response time maintained

Q3 2024: Scale Operations

Expanded to 5 cities, launched Palestine support campaign, and increased orphan care capacity to 25 children.

Impact: 15,000+ lives touched across multiple campaigns

Q4 2024: Infrastructure Development

Groundbreaking for permanent orphanage facility, technology platform launch, and volunteer training program.

Current: 60% funding secured for permanent facility
